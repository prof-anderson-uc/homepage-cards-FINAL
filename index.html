What Are Feature Cards?

Feature cards are small content blocks often used on homepages to preview important parts of a website. They help break information into smaller, easier-to-scan sections.

A feature card often includes an icon, a heading, and a short paragraph. Cards can be used to highlight services, sections of a site, key ideas, or important next steps for the visitor.
